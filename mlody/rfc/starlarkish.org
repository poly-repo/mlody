* Aggregates
While scalars are considered atomic (e.g. they may or not be present,
but when they're available we assume they're entirely available and
"frozen"), the same is not true for aggregates. Elements in an
aggregate may not be all available, not all available yet or change
value over time (TT: this is trick and we may eant to disallow it)
